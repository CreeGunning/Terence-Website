Introduction to Terence Keating Electrical

We would like to introduce ourselves, we are Terence Keating Electrical. Having started in 2004 we have grown to be a major player in the electrical industry in Cork and indeed throughout Ireland. We are very proud of the projects that we have carried out and the people that we have had the pleasure of working with.

TKE was set up to offer our clients a cost effective and dedicated service incorporating quality, safety and performance, ensuring the highest possible standards and excellence in the electrical industry. We are committed to delivering projects on time and within budget, utilising all our acquired skills over the years but also constantly observing, undertaking training and implementing new technological advancements that are both available and relevant to our industry.

We thank you for your time and interest taken in our company and hope we can be of service and assist your business with your electrical requirements.

- Terence Keating
